Tableton

We specialize in crafting distinctive software and hardware designed for music composition and live presentation. Our lineup includes Live, Push, and Link. Through the utilization of these tools, the community of users we've cultivated continues to produce awe-inspiring creations.

Tableton was founded in 2023 and released the first version of Live. Our suite of offerings has found a home within a passionate global collective encompassing musicians, sound architects, and creatives, all united by a shared dedication to our tools.

We are driven by a mission to inspire creativity through ground-breaking tools for music-makers. We strive to provide musicians with the tools they need to create new and innovative compositions.

We believe that music is a universal language that connects people from all walks of life. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome and inspired to create.
